Guidebooks

Comprehensive delivery guides for PMO, VRO, and Admin teams. Each guidebook provides the methodology, processes, standards, and templates needed to deliver with excellence from Day 1.

Our Delivery Guidebooks

These guidebooks serve as the single source of truth for how PwC PH Managed Services PMO operates. Whether you’re onboarding to a new engagement, ramping up a new team member, or looking for process standards — start here.

PMO Guidebook

Project Management Office

The definitive guide to delivering PMO services within Managed Services. Covers the full engagement lifecycle from mobilisation to close-out, including governance frameworks, reporting standards, and quality assurance protocols.

What’s Inside:

  • Engagement Setup & Onboarding — Team mobilisation, access provisioning, stakeholder mapping, kick-off preparation
  • Governance Framework — Decision-making structures, escalation paths, RACI matrices, tollgate criteria
  • Project Planning & Scheduling — WBS development, milestone planning, critical path analysis, dependency mapping
  • RAID Management — Risk identification & scoring, assumption tracking, issue resolution workflows, dependency management
  • Status Reporting — Weekly/monthly reporting cadence, RAG status definitions, dashboard standards, SteerCo pack preparation
  • Resource & Budget Tracking — Effort tracking, budget burn monitoring, forecast accuracy, utilisation reporting
  • Quality Assurance — QA checklists, peer review processes, delivery standards, continuous improvement
  • Stakeholder Management — Communication plans, engagement cadence, relationship management, feedback loops
  • Transition & Close-out — Knowledge transfer, lessons learned, engagement wrap-up, artefact archival

PMO Admin Guidebook

PMO Administration

The operational handbook for PMO Admin team members. Provides detailed procedures, checklists, and standards for all administrative functions that keep engagements running smoothly and efficiently.

What’s Inside:

  • Meeting Coordination — Scheduling protocols, agenda preparation, minutes capture, action item tracking, follow-up procedures
  • Document Management — Naming conventions, folder structures, version control, SharePoint standards, archival policies
  • Onboarding Support — New joiner checklists, access request workflows, equipment provisioning, orientation guides
  • Engagement Logistics — Travel coordination, room bookings, catering, event logistics, visitor management
  • Time & Expense Tracking — Timesheet submission procedures, expense claim workflows, approval processes
  • Communication Support — Distribution list management, newsletter coordination, announcement templates
  • Data Entry & Maintenance — Tracker updates, data hygiene, consolidation processes, reconciliation procedures
  • Offboarding Procedures — Access removal checklists, equipment return, knowledge transfer, exit documentation

VRO Guidebook

Value Realisation Office

The strategic guidebook for delivering Value Realisation services. Outlines the end-to-end VRO methodology — from benefits identification through to value tracking, reporting, and stakeholder communication of outcomes achieved.

What’s Inside:

  • VRO Fundamentals — What is a VRO, why it matters, how it fits within programme governance, value vs. output mindset
  • Benefits Identification — Workshop facilitation, benefits discovery techniques, categorisation frameworks (financial, non-financial, strategic)
  • Benefits Mapping — Linking deliverables to outcomes, benefits dependency networks, benefits realisation maps
  • Business Case Support — Cost-benefit analysis templates, ROI modelling, investment case narratives, scenario planning
  • Value Tracking & Measurement — KPI definition, baseline setting, measurement methodology, data collection protocols
  • Benefits Reporting — Dashboard design, executive reporting, variance analysis, benefits realisation status
  • Stakeholder Value Narratives — Translating data into compelling stories, board-level presentations, value communication
  • Post-Implementation Review — Benefits sustainability assessment, lessons learned, continuous value optimisation

How to Use These Guidebooks

Follow this recommended approach to get the most from our delivery guidebooks.

New to the Team?

Start by reading the guidebook relevant to your role (PMO, VRO, or Admin) end-to-end. Pay special attention to the onboarding and engagement setup sections.

  • Read your role’s guidebook fully
  • Complete the onboarding checklist
  • Download key templates
  • Meet your engagement team

On an Active Engagement?

Use the guidebooks as a reference manual. Jump to the specific process area you need and follow the documented standards.

  • Reference specific process areas
  • Follow reporting standards
  • Use prescribed templates
  • Escalate per documented paths

In a BD Conversation?

Reference the guidebooks to demonstrate our structured methodology and delivery rigour. The table of contents alone showcases the depth of our capability.

  • Showcase our methodology
  • Demonstrate delivery maturity
  • Reference specific capabilities
  • Build client confidence
