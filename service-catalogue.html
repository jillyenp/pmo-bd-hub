PMO as a Service

Project Management Office as a Service

Our flagship offering. PMO provides end-to-end project governance and delivery support for complex transformation programmes. From planning through execution to close-out, we embed experienced PMO professionals who operate as an extension of the client's team.

We bring a structured methodology, standardised templates, and disciplined reporting cadence that ensures every programme stays on track, on budget, and aligned to strategic objectives.

Core Capabilities

  • Programme & Project Planning — WBS development, milestone planning, critical path analysis, resource planning, dependency mapping
  • Governance & Tollgates — Governance framework design, decision structures, RACI development, tollgate management, escalation protocols
  • RAID Management — Risk registers, assumption logs, issue tracking, dependency management, mitigation action tracking
  • Status Reporting — Weekly/monthly reports, RAG status management, executive dashboards, SteerCo packs, variance analysis
  • Resource & Budget Management — Effort tracking, budget monitoring, forecast management, utilisation reporting, change request processing
  • Stakeholder Management — Communication plans, stakeholder maps, engagement cadence, relationship management
  • Quality Assurance — QA checklists, peer reviews, deliverable standards, continuous improvement tracking
  • Transition & Close-out — Knowledge transfer, lessons learned, artefact archival, handover documentation

VRO as a Service

Value Realisation Office as a Service

Value Realisation is the bridge between programme delivery and business outcomes. Our VRO service ensures that the promised benefits of any transformation are identified, tracked, measured, and reported — giving stakeholders confidence that their investment is delivering returns.

We go beyond traditional PMO by focusing specifically on what value is being created, not just what tasks are being completed. This outcomes-oriented approach differentiates PwC in the market and strengthens client relationships.

Core Capabilities

  • Benefits Identification & Discovery — Workshop facilitation, stakeholder interviews, benefits categorisation (financial, non-financial, strategic, compliance)
  • Benefits Mapping & Dependencies — Benefits dependency networks, deliverable-to-outcome linkages, benefits realisation maps
  • Business Case Support — Cost-benefit analysis, ROI modelling, payback calculations, investment case narratives, scenario analysis
  • KPI Definition & Baseline Setting — KPI selection, measurement methodology, baseline data collection, target setting
  • Value Tracking & Measurement — Ongoing benefits tracking, variance analysis, realisation rate monitoring, leading/lagging indicators
  • Benefits Reporting & Dashboards — Executive dashboards, benefits realisation reports, board-level presentations, trend analysis
  • Stakeholder Value Narratives — Translating data into compelling value stories, ROI communication, success case development
  • Post-Implementation Review — Benefits sustainability assessment, value optimisation opportunities, lessons for future programmes

PMO Admin as a Service

PMO Administration as a Service

Every high-performing programme needs a strong administrative backbone. PMO Admin provides the operational support that keeps engagements running smoothly — from meeting logistics and document management to onboarding coordination and data maintenance.

By centralising administrative functions with our trained team, engagement leads can redirect senior resources to higher-value activities while maintaining operational discipline and consistency.

Core Capabilities

  • Meeting Management — Scheduling, agenda preparation, minutes capture, action tracking, calendar management, recurring cadence setup
  • Document Management — SharePoint administration, folder structure setup, naming conventions, version control, access management
  • Onboarding & Offboarding — New joiner checklists, access provisioning, equipment coordination, orientation scheduling, exit procedures
  • Engagement Logistics — Travel booking, room reservations, event coordination, catering, visitor management
  • Time & Expense Administration — Timesheet monitoring, expense processing, approval workflows, compliance reporting
  • Data Entry & Tracker Maintenance — Regular tracker updates, data consolidation, reconciliation, reporting support
  • Communication Support — Distribution list management, newsletter formatting, announcement coordination, template maintenance
  • General PMO Support — Ad hoc requests, research tasks, presentation formatting, document review coordination

Engagement Lifecycle

Every Managed Services engagement follows a structured five-phase lifecycle that ensures quality, consistency, and client satisfaction from start to finish.

1

Scope & Qualify

Opportunity identification, requirements gathering, scope definition, pricing, and proposal development.

2

Mobilise

Team selection, onboarding, access provisioning, tooling setup, governance design, and engagement kick-off.

3

Deliver

Steady-state execution — reporting cadence, RAID management, benefits tracking, stakeholder engagement, QA reviews.

4

Optimise

Continuous improvement, process refinement, automation opportunities, team upskilling, and service expansion.

5

Transition

Knowledge transfer, lessons learned documentation, artefact handover, engagement closure, and relationship continuity.
