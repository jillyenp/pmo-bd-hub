Elevator Pitches

Ready-to-use pitch statements for different audiences and contexts. Tailor as needed, but keep the core value proposition intact.

30-Second Overview Pitch

For: Any audience — quick introduction

We deliver 30–40% cost savings versus onshore delivery while maintaining PwC's quality standards. With 19 ready-to-deploy templates and a proven Enable–Execute–Evolve methodology, we can mobilise a fully operational team within 2–4 weeks — giving you governance, reporting, and delivery assurance from Day 1."

PMO Pitch

For: Engagement leaders needing PMO support

"Our PMO service gives you a fully equipped project management team — project planners, RAID managers, reporting analysts, and governance specialists — operating from the Philippines at 30–40% lower cost. We handle everything from project planning and milestone tracking to SteerCo packs and risk escalation. You get PwC-quality delivery with standardised processes and 50+ battle-tested templates, so your onshore team can focus on strategy and client relationships rather than administrative overhead."

For: Partners / Directors (strategic framing)

"Think of PMO as your delivery backbone. We provide the operational rigour — governance, RAID management, reporting cadence, quality assurance — that keeps complex programmes on track. Our teams have supported $50M+ ERP transformations, multi-year cloud migrations, and cross-border regulatory programmes. We scale from 3 to 15 FTEs based on your needs, and our structured methodology means we're productive within weeks, not months."

VRO Pitch

For: Clients focused on outcomes and ROI

"Every transformation promises benefits — but how many actually track and prove them? Our Value Realisation Office service does exactly that. We identify, map, baseline, track, and report on the benefits your programme is delivering. From KPI dashboards to board-level value narratives, we give stakeholders confidence that their investment is generating real returns. We've tracked over $12M in validated benefits across a single engagement."

For: Proposal / bid inclusion

"The PwC VRO as a Service offering provides a dedicated benefits management function embedded within the programme. Our team will establish a benefits framework, define KPIs and baselines, conduct ongoing tracking and measurement, and deliver regular value realisation reports to the steering committee. This ensures the programme remains outcomes-focused and that stakeholders have clear visibility of the value being generated throughout the engagement lifecycle."

For: Engagement managers with admin overhead

"How much of your senior team's time is spent on meeting logistics, document management, and onboarding paperwork? Our PMO Admin service handles all of that — meeting scheduling and minutes, SharePoint management, onboarding coordination, travel logistics, and engagement housekeeping. At 40%+ cost savings, you free up your senior people to focus on delivery and client engagement while we keep the operational engine running smoothly."

Combined Services Pitch

For: Large programmes needing integrated support

"For your most complex programmes, we offer an integrated PMO + VRO + Admin package. You get the full spectrum — project governance and delivery tracking from the PMO team, benefits identification and value reporting from the VRO team, and seamless operational support from the Admin team. PwC Philippines — Managed Services

Who We Are

PwC Philippines Managed Services is a dedicated, scalable delivery centre providing project management, value realisation, and administrative support services to PwC engagement teams globally. Operating from Manila, our team of trained professionals supports programmes across APAC, EMEA, and the Americas.

What We Deliver

  • PMO: End-to-end project governance, planning, RAID management, status reporting, and delivery assurance
  • VRO: Benefits identification, tracking, measurement, KPI dashboards, and value realisation reporting
  • PMO Admin: Meeting management, document control, onboarding, logistics, and engagement operations

Why Choose Us

  • Cost Efficient: 30–40% savings vs. onshore delivery without quality compromise
  • Rapid Mobilisation: Teams operational within 1–4 weeks with pre-built templates and frameworks

  • Evolve: Continuous improvement, lessons learned, process automation, capability uplift

  • 3 Service Lines: PMO, VRO, and Admin for comprehensive programme support
  • Global Coverage: Supporting engagements across APAC, EMEA, and Americas time zones

How to Position PMO / VRO

Practical guidance for different BD scenarios. Match the positioning to the client's pain points and decision-makers.

Talking to a Partner

Lead with: Cost efficiency and scalability

"We can staff your PMO at 30–40% lower cost with pre-trained teams who are productive in weeks. This improves your engagement margin while maintaining PwC quality."

  • Emphasise margin improvement
  • Highlight rapid mobilisation
  • Reference similar engagements
  • Position as low-risk, high-reward
  • Offer to set up a brief intro call with MS leadership

Talking to an Engagement Manager

Lead with: Delivery quality and template readiness

"We bring 19 tested templates and a structured methodology. Your team won't need to build anything from scratch — we deploy proven artefacts and processes from Day 1."

  • Show the Templates Library
  • Walk through the PMO Guidebook
  • Demonstrate reporting standards
  • Emphasise QA and consistency
  • Offer a pilot engagement

Talking to a Client

Lead with: Outcomes, value, and track record

"Our dedicated team ensures your programme stays on track with disciplined governance and transparent reporting. We've supported similar transformations delivering measurable cost savings and on-time delivery."

  • Focus on outcomes, not inputs
  • Use case study examples
  • Highlight value realisation capability
  • Demonstrate governance maturity
  • Offer credentials presentation

Do's

  • Position MS as a strategic capability, not just "cheap resources"
  • Emphasise quality, methodology, and templates
  • Use specific numbers — "30–40% savings", "19 templates", "2-week mobilisation"
  • Reference real case studies and engagement outcomes
  • Highlight the Enable–Execute–Evolve framework
  • Tailor the pitch to the audience's pain points
  • Offer to arrange a call with MS service leads
  • Share the Service Catalogue one-pager as leave-behind
  • Start with a small pilot if there's hesitation

Don'ts

  • Don't position as "offshoring" or "outsourcing" — use "Managed Services"
  • Don't lead with cost alone — lead with quality and capability
  • Don't promise capabilities we don't currently offer
  • Don't skip stakeholder alignment before proposing MS
  • Don't underestimate change management needed for MS adoption
  • Don't present MS as a replacement for onshore teams — it's an extension
  • Don't share internal rate cards with clients directly
  • Don't forget to involve MS leadership in large opportunity discussions
  • Don't over-commit on timelines without checking resource availability

BD Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions that come up in BD conversations — and how to answer them confidently.

What exactly does Managed Services do?

PwC's Managed Services goes beyond individual staff augmentation or body shopping. We offer additional value with innovative tools and digital assets, access to our Change Academy, and invest in our relationship with clients — their success is our success.

We support clients across a variety of engagement models:

  • Flexible Resource — Deploying targeted, skilled resources to address specific capability gaps, bringing best practice where needed.
  • Managed Programme / Capacity — Deploying a full project/programme team, aligned to existing ways of working to deliver discrete packages of work.
  • As a Service — Deploying a full change service, directed by PwC to deliver extensive packages of work using PwC's proprietary service catalogue.

PMO is one of the service lines within Managed Services. Under PMO, we offer three core capabilities: PMO as a Service (project governance, planning, RAID, reporting), VRO (benefits tracking, value measurement, KPI dashboards), and PMO Admin (meeting management, document control, onboarding support). We have the flexibility to scale and transition into various change models to best suit client needs.

How much can we save compared to onshore delivery?

Typically 30–40% for PMO and VRO services, and 40%+ for Admin services compared to equivalent onshore resourcing. These savings come from our Philippines-based delivery model while maintaining PwC's global quality standards and methodology.

How quickly can you mobilise a team?

Admin teams: 1–2 weeks. PMO teams: 2–4 weeks. VRO teams: 2–3 weeks. Our rapid mobilisation is possible because we have pre-trained professionals and 19 ready-to-deploy templates and frameworks. We don't start from scratch — we start from PwC standards.

What's the minimum engagement size?

We can start with as little as 1 FTE for Admin or 2–3 FTEs for PMO/VRO. For a meaningful PMO impact, we typically recommend a minimum of 3 FTEs. We're flexible — if you want to start with a small pilot and scale up, that works perfectly.

What time zones do you cover?

We currently cover SEA, Australia, and New Zealand time zones. However, we are open and flexible to cater to other regions including EMEA, other APAC markets, and the Americas based on engagement requirements. Our team adapts to client working hours to ensure seamless collaboration.

How do you maintain quality and consistency?

Quality is built into everything we do: (1) Standardised templates and artefacts — 19 across all services. (2) Defined processes documented in our Guidebooks and Guidebooks. (3) QA checklists and peer review protocols for every deliverable. (4) Regular delivery assurance reviews. (5) Continuous improvement culture with retrospectives and lessons learned.

What tools and platforms do you support?

Our team is proficient in core project management tools including Microsoft Project, Excel, PowerPoint, SharePoint, and Teams. We also have experience with Jira, ServiceNow, and Monday.com.

For tools like SmartSheet, Power BI, Azure DevOps, Tableau, and Confluence — while not all team members are currently proficient, we are committed to upskilling as needed based on engagement requirements. Our team has a strong track record of quickly learning and adapting to new tools and platforms.

Is this the same as offshoring?

No. Managed Services is different from traditional offshoring. We provide a managed, governed service with defined deliverables, quality standards, and PwC methodology — not just "bodies in seats." Our teams follow PwC's delivery framework, use standardised templates, and undergo regular quality assurance reviews. Think of us as a service provider within the PwC network, not an outsource vendor.

Can you work on confidential / regulated engagements?

Yes. Our team members operate under PwC's global confidentiality and data protection standards. We follow all required independence, ethics, and security protocols. For highly regulated engagements, we work with the engagement team to ensure compliance with specific data handling, access, and clearance requirements.

How does onboarding work for a new engagement?

We follow our Enable–Execute–Evolve model: (1) Enable — scope definition, team selection, access provisioning, governance design, template deployment, kick-off meeting. (2) Execute — steady-state delivery with defined reporting cadence and escalation paths. (3) Evolve — continuous improvement and refinement based on engagement feedback. The Enable phase typically takes 1–4 weeks depending on service complexity.

What if the engagement scope changes?

We're designed to be flexible. We can scale up or down based on engagement needs — add more team members for peak phases, reduce during quieter periods. Scope changes are managed through our standard change request process, and we work collaboratively with the engagement lead to ensure smooth transitions.

Conversation Starters

Use these questions to open BD discussions and identify opportunities for Managed Services.

Identifying PMO Need

Ask the engagement team or client:

  • "How are you currently managing project governance and reporting?"
  • "Do you have a dedicated PMO team, or are consultants handling this on the side?"
  • "How much time does your senior team spend on status reports and RAID management?"
  • "Would you benefit from a structured PMO that's operational in 2–4 weeks?"

Identifying VRO Need

Ask the engagement team or client:

  • "Is anyone currently tracking whether the programme is delivering its promised benefits?"
  • "How do you report value back to the board or steering committee?"
  • "Do you have KPIs and baselines defined for the expected outcomes?"
  • "Would a dedicated Value Realisation Office strengthen your business case?"

Identifying Admin Need

Ask the engagement team or client:

  • "Who's handling meeting logistics, minutes, and document management today?"
  • "Are senior resources spending time on admin tasks instead of delivery?"
  • "Do you need support onboarding new team members or managing SharePoint?"
  • "Would freeing up 30% of your admin overhead let you focus on higher-value work?"
