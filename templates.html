How to Use These Templates

Follow these guidelines to get the most value from the template library.

1. Download & Customise

Click the link to open the template in SharePoint. Download or create a copy, then customise it with your engagement-specific details — client name, dates, team members, and project context.

2. Apply Client Branding

Where required, apply the client's branding (logos, colours) while maintaining PwC structural standards. Keep the content framework intact — it reflects our proven methodology.

3. Iterate & Improve

Templates are living documents. Update them as the engagement evolves. If you develop improvements, share them with the MS team so we can uplift the standard template for everyone.
